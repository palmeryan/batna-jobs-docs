# Job Preferences

## Candidate profile

Summarize the candidate in a few sentences.

## Workplace constraints

State the user's hard requirements and softer preferences for remote, hybrid, onsite, time-zone, or travel expectations.

## Geography constraints

State the user's hard geography limits and any softer regional preferences.

## IC vs. management preference

State whether the user wants IC roles only, manager roles only, or is open to both, including any nuance about player-coach roles.

## Strengths to prioritize

List the strengths the review agent should actively look for in job descriptions.

## Preferred verticals

### top_priority

List the strongest-fit verticals or problem spaces.

### open

List areas that are acceptable but not preferred.

### none

List areas that should count against a role unless something else is exceptionally strong.

## Role-level hard exclusions

List role patterns, org patterns, or responsibility patterns that should be treated as automatic no-go signals.

## Notes

Capture ambiguity, tradeoffs, and any judgment calls the review skill should make carefully.
