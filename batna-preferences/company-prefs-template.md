# Company Preferences

## Hard exclusions

List industries, business models, or company types that should be treated as automatic no-go signals.

## Business-model notes

Call out business-model patterns that are especially attractive or especially unattractive.

## Optional stage or size preferences

Note any clear company-stage or team-size preferences that should matter at company review time.

## Geo or compliance constraints

Include only company-level constraints here, such as "must hire in the US" if that is best judged at the company level.

## Notes

Capture nuance that should shape the company-stage decision without turning into role-level logic.
